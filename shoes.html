75 Greatest NBA Players of All Time

My Ranking of The Top 10 Greatest

#1 Michael Jordan

  • Career: 1984-93, 1995-98 Chicago Bulls; 2001-03 Washington Wizards
  • 6x NBA Champion, 6x Finals MVP
  • 5x MVP

#2 LeBron James

  • Career: 2003-10, 2014-18 Cleveland Cavs, 2010-14 Miami Heat, 2018-present Los Angeles Lakers
  • 4x NBA Champion, 4x Finals MVP
  • 4x MVP

#3 Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

  • Career: 1969-75 Milwaukee Bucks, 1975-89 Los Angeles Lakers
  • 6x NBA Champion, 2x Finals MVP
  • 6x MVP

#4 Magic Johnson

  • Career: 1979-91, 1996, 1999-2000 Los Angeles Lakers
  • 5x NBA Champion, 3x Finals MVP
  • 3x MVP

#5 Shaquille O'Neal

  • Career: 1992-96 Magic, 1996-04 Lakers, 2004-2008 Heat, 08-09 Suns, 09-10 Cavs, 10-11 Celtics
  • 4x NBA Champion, 3x Finals MVP
  • 1x MVP

#6 Tim Duncan

  • Career: 1997-2016 San Antonio Spurs
  • 5x NBA Champion, 3x Finals MVP
  • 2x MVP

#7 Kobe Bryant

  • Career: 1996-2016 Los Angeles Lakers
  • 5x NBA Champion, 2x Finals MVP
  • 1x MVP

#8 Bill Russell

  • Career: 1956-1969 Boston Celtics
  • 11x NBA Champion
  • 5x MVP

#9 Wilt Chamberlain

  • Career: 1959-65 Philadelphia/SF Warriors, 1965-68 Philadelphia 76ers, 1968-73 Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2x NBA Champion, 1x MVP
  • 4x MVP

#10 Stephen Curry

  • Career: 2009-present Golden State Warriors
  • 3x NBA Champion
  • 2x MVP
